Our Story

From a small team of passionate developers to a full-service digital agency.

Mobintix Infotech began with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between complex technology and business success. Founded in Surat, India, we started as a boutique web development firm delivering high-quality custom websites.

Over the years, we have evolved into a comprehensive digital solutions provider. Our team grew, and so did our expertise. Today, we specialize not just in web development, but also in mobile apps, cloud infrastructure, and strategic digital marketing. However, our core philosophy remains unchanged: Client Success is Our Success.

We believe in building long-term partnerships. We don't just write code; we solve problems. Whether you are a startup looking to launch your first product or an enterprise seeking digital transformation, Mobintix is your trusted partner.
